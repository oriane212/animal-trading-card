Red Panda

With an estimated global population of less than 10,000, red pandas are classified as endangered.

  • Conservation Status: Endangered
  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Life Span: 8-10 years
  • Habitat: Temperate forests

The red panda, or ailurus fulgens, is a mammal native to the temperate forests of the Himalayas. It is considered an endangered species and its population continues to decline. It has an average life span of eight to ten years.
